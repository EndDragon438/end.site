     ahhhhh i actually gotta describe myself oh no. here goes nothing

     hiya! i'm end, or end draconis if you must. i'm a girl, a dragon, and a lil bit robot on occasion. my field of expertise is in computer science, where i particularly enjoy software development and direct tech support. i pick up and drop hobbies all the time, i really enjoy learning new skills. i've tried my hand at embroidery (hope to get better), web development (clearly), wiki management (dropped now), drawing (getting better!), 3d art (modelling, sculpting, texturing, animating, the works. also getting better!), and probably more that i've forgotten. i really want to learn more about making music and drawing, i like having an artistic outlet (though i do see programming as an artistic kinda thing).

     i started this website a few years back as an escape from the swift decline of social media. i've spent a lot of my life on the computer, and the internet, and in recent years i've been trying to kinda get away from the mainstream internet to something that's all my own, and just for fun. i use this site to toy around with web design, share things i'm working on, and (hopefully) to share my thoughts 'n writing.

     if you'd like to let me know about an issue with the site, or just chat, feel free to contact me anywhere in the Contact tab!

  • Site

    Site

    Written in VSCode, Kate, and VSCodium over the years. Serverless server-side includes provided by a custom Python mini static site generator. Source code hosted on Github, and distributed from there to Neocities. end.site began on April 11, 2024.
