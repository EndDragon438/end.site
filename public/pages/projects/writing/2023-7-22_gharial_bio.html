About:
Name: Gharial
Tribe: Mudwing
Location: 1/3 up the Diamond Spray River
Pronouns: she/her
Queerities: cis, lesbian
Age: 14 (32 human)

Biography:
 Gharial is the Bigwings of her clutch, and led her sibs through the end of the Succession War. All four survived the war, and after they were all lost about where to go next. In the end, they decided to go off in their own separate directions for some time, promising to reunite in the future. Gharial decides to start a small town up the Diamond Spray River, built specifically for veterans of the 20 year war. She brings some friends along with her to get the town off the ground. Her sibs know of her plans, and in their respective journeys send the friends they meet to the town. Gharial slowly grows her town, and learns a lot along the way. Eventually even finding a partner who can understand her, and possibly teach her more about when to ask for help.
